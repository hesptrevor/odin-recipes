Snake Juice Recipe

Snake Juice is an electrolyte beverage designed by Cole Robinson.
It is used to maintain electrolytes during fasts. Drink as much as you want while fasting.

Ingredients

  • 2l water
  • 1 tsp of potassium chloride
  • 1/2 tsp of sodium chloride
  • 1 tsp of sodium bicarbonate

Steps

  1. Mix well
  2. Enjoy
